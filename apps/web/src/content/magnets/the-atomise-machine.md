# The Atomise Machine

*Film once, publish six. The SlicedLabs system for turning one recording into a
week of content across every surface — without burning out. The marketing pillar,
given away.*

---

## The core idea
You don't make "a YouTube video." You make **one source recording** and atomise it
into a week of assets. The long video is the quarry; everything else is cut from it.
This is why a solo operator can out-publish a team: leverage, not hustle.

The data backs it — Food & Beverage is the **4th-largest category on YouTube**
(+32% YoY views, Q1 2025), and **99% of top food channels are creator-driven**, not
brands (Pixability). The field rewards individuals who show up consistently.

## One recording → six (plus) pieces

```
        ┌─────────────────────────────┐
        │   1 SOURCE RECORDING        │
        │  (a cook + a build update)  │
        └──────────────┬──────────────┘
   ┌──────────┬────────┼────────┬──────────┬───────────┐
   ▼          ▼        ▼        ▼          ▼           ▼
 1 long     3–6      1 news-   3–5       1 blog/     1 carousel
 YouTube    Shorts/  letter    tweets/   recipe      / IG post
 video      Reels/   issue     threads   page
            TikToks
```

1. **The long video** — the full cook + the build update. Home base.
2. **3–6 short clips** — the best 20–60s moments → Shorts, Reels, TikTok.
3. **The newsletter issue** — the receipts in writing, links to the video.
4. **3–5 social posts** — quotes, the number, the lesson → X, IG, Threads, LinkedIn.
5. **A blog or recipe page** — the searchable, evergreen version (SEO).
6. **A carousel / still** — the one-image takeaway.

## The weekly rhythm (maps to your calendar)
- **Mon — Film.** One block. Cook segment + build update + B-roll.
- **Tue–Wed — Cut.** Long video + pull the clips.
- **Thu — Publish.** The episode goes live; schedule the week's shorts.
- **Fri — Newsletter.** The owned list goes out; the algorithm can't throttle it.
- **All week — Atomise.** Drip the shorts + posts from Thursday's cut.

## The toolchain (own the commodity, build the edge)
- **Record:** OBS Studio (free) · a good mic (Shure MV7+) · DJI/RØDE for field.
- **Edit:** Descript (text-based) or CapCut (free finishing).
- **Clip:** OpusClip / Spikes Studio (long video → 30+ clips, auto-ranked).
- **Email:** beehiiv (the owned list).
- **Schedule:** your automation spine (n8n / Activepieces) drips it out.

*You rent the editor and the clipper. You build the one thing no subscription
gives you: the audience and the pipeline.*

## Why it compounds
Every recording feeds six surfaces; every surface points back to the owned list;
the list converts at **~8% vs social's ~3%**. The machine turns one hour of filming
into a week of reach — and the reach into an asset you own.
