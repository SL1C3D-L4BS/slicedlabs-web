# Spokane food truck — the real licensing path (2026)

> Real, source-cited. Costs marked **verify** must be confirmed with the agency
> before you rely on them. Fees change; this is a map, not legal advice.

## The sequence (do not reorder)
1. **Form the LLC** — WA Secretary of State (~$200 online).
2. **WA Business License / UBI** — Dept. of Revenue Business Licensing Service ($50).
3. **Submit unit plans to L&I** — review can take **8+ weeks**; start before you build.
4. **Submit mobile-unit plans to SRHD** — plan-review packet; lock the commissary.
5. **Build / outfit the truck.**
6. **L&I cover inspection → final inspection → insignia** on the unit.
7. **SRHD final inspection → mobile food unit permit** issued.
8. **Spokane Fire inspection** (propane / hood suppression / extinguishers).
9. **Food Worker Cards** ($10/person) + **1 Certified Food Protection Manager**.
10. **Operate.** File DOR returns.

## The rules that shape the build
- **Commissary is required** (WAC 246-215): the truck returns **daily** to an
  approved commissary/servicing area for prep, water, wastewater, washing,
  storage. Exemption only if fully self-contained **and** SRHD approves in writing.
- **14-day rule (SRHD):** apply ≥14 days before operating or fees may double.
- **L&I two inspections:** "cover" (before walls close) then "final" → insignia.
  Plumbing/electrical/LP work must be done by licensed trades.
- **Food Protection Manager** (WAC 246-215-02107): ≥1 per establishment with a
  current ANSI/ANAB credential (e.g. ServSafe Manager). In effect since 2023.

## 2026 changes (don't miss)
- **City of Spokane eliminated its mobile-food-vendor permit** (Ord. C36863,
  May 2026) — the old $60 city permit + property-owner-consent requirement are
  gone. State + SRHD permits still required.
- **L&I fees rise 2026-07-01** (insignia $51.80→$55.10, inspection $105→$111.80/hr).
- **WSAFM statewide reciprocal fire inspection** (since 2024): one fire
  inspection is recognized across participating WA departments.

## Taxes
- **Spokane combined sales tax: 9.0%** (6.5% state + 2.5% local) — verify by exact
  location. Food trucks almost always exceed the 75% prepared-food threshold
  (RCW 82.08.0293) → collect sales tax on **all** food sales. B&O = Retailing.
