# SlicedLabs — research & data hub

The single source of truth for the **real** data behind SlicedLabs. Everything the
website, Discord, and the briefings cite pulls from here. Researched 2026-06-20 with
cited sources.

**The honest-data law:** real, sourced facts only. Nothing fabricated. Anything that
needs a local phone call to confirm is marked **verify** — never presented as settled.

## The docs
- **[spokane-food-truck-licensing.md](spokane-food-truck-licensing.md)** — the real
  permit sequence, agencies, costs, 2026 changes. The path to legally opening.
- **[pnw-sourcing.md](pnw-sourcing.md)** — real farms, food hubs, markets, breweries,
  Inland NW seasonality. Who supplies the food.

  with honest caveats on what NOT to claim.
- **[outreach-targets.md](outreach-targets.md)** — the working call-list: who to reach
